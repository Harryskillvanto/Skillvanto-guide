📝 Subject Lines

Replace [Role] with the actual job title. Rotate — never use same subject twice to same person.

1[Role] – Candidate Referral
e.g. Physician – Candidate Referral | Pharmacist – Candidate Referral | LMSW – Candidate Referral
2[Role] – Direct Hire Candidates
e.g. Clinical Therapist – Direct Hire Candidates | Dental Hygienist – Direct Hire Candidates
3Qualified [Role] Candidates Available
e.g. Qualified Pharmacist Candidates Available | Qualified Physician Candidates Available
4[Role] – Staffing Support
e.g. LMSW – Staffing Support | Pharmacy Tech – Staffing Support
5Direct Hire – [Role] Candidates
e.g. Direct Hire – Dental Hygienist Candidates | Direct Hire – Social Worker Candidates
6[Role] – Candidate Profiles
e.g. Pulmonologist – Candidate Profiles | Occupational Therapist – Candidate Profiles
7[Role] – Direct Placement
e.g. Registered Nurse – Direct Placement | Social Worker – Direct Placement
8Direct Hire Referral – [Role]
e.g. Direct Hire Referral – Physician | Direct Hire Referral – Clinical Therapist
9[Role] – Qualified Candidates
e.g. Pharmacy Technician – Qualified Candidates | Physician – Qualified Candidates
10[Role] – Referral Candidates
e.g. BCBA – Referral Candidates | Medical Laboratory Scientist – Referral Candidates
💡 Rule: Use a different subject line each time you email the same person.

✉️ Short Templates

3-4 lines. Quick and direct. Max 8 emails per template per day.

1Short — Direct Placement Direct Placement

Subject: [Role] – Candidate Referral
Hi [First Name], We are Skillvanto, a staffing company specializing in healthcare placements. We have a few qualified [Role] candidates available for direct placement — would you be interested in reviewing their profiles?

2Short — Direct Hire Direct Hire

Subject: Direct Hire – [Role] Candidates
Hi [First Name], We are Skillvanto, a healthcare staffing company focused on direct hire placements. We have a few [Role] candidates available — would you be open to reviewing their profiles?

3Short — Direct Hire Referral Direct Hire Referral

Subject: Direct Hire Referral – [Role]
Hi [First Name], We are Skillvanto, a staffing company currently working with qualified [Role] candidates available for direct hire referral. Please let me know if you would be interested in reviewing their profiles.
💡 Never use the same template twice in one day. Rotate across all templates.

📄 Medium Templates

5-6 lines. More detail with personal introduction.

4Medium — Direct Hire Direct Hire

Subject: [Role] – Direct Hire Candidates
Hi [First Name], I hope this message finds you well. I am reaching out from Skillvanto, a staffing company specializing in healthcare placements. We are currently working with qualified [Role] candidates who are available for direct hire and actively looking for their next opportunity. Would you be open to reviewing their profiles?

5Medium — Referral Referral

Subject: [Role] – Referral Candidates
Hi [First Name], My name is [Your Name] and I am contacting you from Skillvanto, a healthcare staffing company. We have a few qualified [Role] candidates available for referral who have relevant experience and are ready to interview. I wanted to check if you would be interested in reviewing their profiles — please let me know and I will send their details right away.

6Medium — Direct Placement Direct Placement

Subject: Qualified [Role] Candidates Available
Hi [First Name], My name is [Your Name] from Skillvanto, a staffing company specializing in healthcare placements. We are currently working with a few qualified [Role] candidates who are actively looking for direct placement opportunities. Would you be open to reviewing their profiles?

7Medium — Direct Hire Direct Hire

Subject: [Role] – Direct Hire Candidates
Hi [First Name], I am reaching out from Skillvanto, a healthcare staffing company focused on direct hire placements. We have qualified [Role] candidates with relevant experience currently available and ready to interview. Would you be interested in reviewing their profiles?

8Medium — Referral Referral

Subject: [Role] – Candidate Referral
Hi [First Name], My name is [Your Name] from Skillvanto, a staffing company specializing in healthcare placements. We have a few qualified [Role] candidates available for referral — all with relevant experience and ready to interview. Please let me know if you would like to review their profiles.

9Medium — Direct Hire Direct Hire

Subject: [Role] – Staffing Support
Hi [First Name], I am [Your Name] from Skillvanto, a healthcare staffing company specializing in direct hire. We are currently working with qualified [Role] candidates available and actively interviewing. Would you be open to reviewing a couple of their profiles?

10Medium — Direct Placement + Referral Direct Placement Referral

Subject: [Role] – Candidate Profiles
Hi [First Name], Reaching out from Skillvanto, a staffing company specializing in healthcare placements. We have a few qualified [Role] candidates available for direct placement referral — all with relevant experience in this field. Would you be interested in reviewing their profiles?
💡 Never use the same template twice in one day. Rotate across all 10 templates.

🔄 Follow Up Templates

Day 3 — Follow Up Option 1

Subject: Following up – [Role] Candidates
Hi [First Name], Just following up on my previous email regarding [Role] candidates. We still have qualified candidates available for direct placement — please let me know if you would like to review their profiles.

Day 3 — Follow Up Option 2

Subject: [Role] Candidates – Follow Up
Hi [First Name], Wanted to check if you had a chance to see my previous email about [Role] candidates available for direct hire referral. Happy to send profiles whenever works for you.

Day 7 — Final Follow Up

Subject: [Role] Candidates – Final Follow Up
Hi [First Name], One last follow up regarding our [Role] candidates available for direct hire referral. Feel free to reach out anytime if you have future hiring needs. Have a great week!
